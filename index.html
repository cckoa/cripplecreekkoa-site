Where is Cripple Creek KOA Holiday located?

Cripple Creek KOA Holiday is located at 2576 County Road 81, Cripple Creek, Colorado 80813. It is about 5 miles from Cripple Creek and about 7.5 miles from Victor.

What types of camping are available?

The campground offers RV sites, cabins, deluxe cabins, and tent sites. This makes it a fit for travelers searching for RV camping near Cripple Creek, cabin stays in the mountains, or tent camping near historic attractions and hiking.

Is the campground near Cripple Creek casinos?

Yes. The campground is a short drive from the Cripple Creek casino district, so guests can combine mountain camping with dining, gaming, and town activities.

What amenities are available at the campground?

Amenities include Wi-Fi, restrooms, showers, laundry, a camp store, pizza service, a dog park, propane, firewood, and EV charging.

When is Cripple Creek KOA Holiday open?

The campground operates seasonally. For 2026, the stated operating season is May 20 through October 18.

What attractions are nearby?

Nearby attractions include Cripple Creek, Victor, Mueller State Park, Florissant Fossil Beds, scenic drives, hiking, wildlife viewing, and other destinations in the Pikes Peak region.
